About Us

Your health is our priority

The stetland Healthcare Group is renowned at home and abroad or medical excellence and attracts world-class Specialist Doctors and Surgeons. Founded in 2011, stetland Healthcare Group has grown over the year with numerous facilities spread across Nigeria with its flagship hospital located in Victoria Island.The stetland Hospital is a leading international healthcare provider in West Africa having been the first independent hospital in Nigeria to be awarded full international accreditation on quality by COHSASA, an international healthcare accreditation body based in South Africa.The stetland has recently been re-accredited for another 3 year (till 2021) following her outstanding success in an audit carried out in June 2018.

Our Services

Overview

Welcome to this section of stetland Hospital’s website. Here you will find details/information about the services we provide across our facilities including details of our consultants. stetland Hospital specialises in the OPD General Practice, O&G, Renal treatment, Paediatrics, Antenatal care. It has excellent clinical and support staff dedicated to ensuring our patients receive the best quality care.

Obstetrics and Gynaecology

At stetland Hospital, we provide compassionate and knowledgeable health care to women as partners in care because your health needs varies throughout your life.
Our team consists of expertise in house consultants, Residents and visiting Consultants. In addition, we have experienced Nurses and support staff....
1. Maternity Services We provide general obstetrical care for both normal and high-risk pregnancies. We provide care before, during and after childbirth. Our services includes pre-conception counseling and screening and obstetrical consultative services.
2. Maternal Fetal Medicine (MFM) The MFM service offers outpatient consultation, ultrasound, genetic counseling and management of medical problems during pregnancy.
3. Gynaecological Services We provide a broad range of basic and specialized medical and surgical services, ranging from primary gynecologic health care which includes diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the female reproductive system, to treating malignancies of the female genital tract.

Paediatrics

Our Paediatric Services at stetland Hospital are based around family-centred care and include comprehensive general Paediatric and sub-speciality medical and surgical services, both at inpatient and outpatient levels.
We provide a wide range of services and facilities for children and young people from birth to 17 years of age with acute and chronic health needs, as well as information and support for their parents....
1.Inpatient Paediatric Services This provides General Paediatric and Specialist services which includes:
1. Special Care Newborn services Unit equipped with Incubators, Phototherapy and Piped oxygen.
2. Paediatric Surgery
3. Orthopaedics
2.The Team
The Service is led by Paediatricians. There are two Consultant Paediatrician supported by Doctors in Postgraduate Training, Medical Officers and House Officers. Our Nursing staff have specialised training in Paediatric Nursing.
3.Accreditation
The Paediatric Service is accredited by the Medical and Dental Council of Nigeria to train House Officers to attain full registration to practice in Nigeria. The Paediatric Service is also accredited by the Faculty of Family Practice/General Practice of the West African and National Postgraduate Medical Colleges to provide Postgraduate Training in Paediatrics.
4.Our Promise
We endeavour to provide the best healthcare for your child whether as an outpatient or inpatient. If admitted to the inpatient ward we will ensure the child’s stay is as short and as anxiety-free as possible.

The Medicine department aims to provide comprehensive, high quality, multidisciplinary, compassionate care in a friendly and supportive environment for people with ailments....

Surgery

Our surgeons deal with ailment that might involve surgery with different specialist Neurosurgeon, Orthopedic and trauma surgeon, General surgeon Urologist and burns and plastic unit ETC. At housebolt Hospital, we provide both comprehensive elective (planned) and emergency services....
1.The Team
Our team consists of Medical Officers, Residents and Consultants of different Surgical units with vast expertise in emergency responses and procedures. They are supported by a team of experienced Nurses and paramedical staff
2.Services
Accident and Emergency
We are able to respond promptly to emergencies in across various specialties which includes orthopaedics and trauma, general surgery, urology, neurosurgery, ENT, ophthalmology, obstetrics and gynecological, paediatrics and medicine.
